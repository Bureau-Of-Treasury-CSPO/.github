## Hi there 👋

This organisation is made for CS and IT interns of Bureau of Treasury - Camarines Sur Pronvincial Office as a way to centralise and manage the projects that will be made throughout the internship period.

## For new interns
Create a team with your preferred name. The default naming convention is the year of your internship + the school + Interns. You can create your own team name especially if you are from different schools. We recommend indicating the year though.

### On building from previous projects
Open the repository you want to build onto. The guide on how to proceed with it is in its README file.

### On creating your own projects
Create your project's repository here in GitHub. Then under your team, add that repo to your team's repository.
